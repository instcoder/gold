Rules/FAQ

Before joining our program you should read the frequently asked questions and answers to them . These F.A.Q. also contain our general rules.

- What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept payments in E-gold and Evocash.

When will I be paid my interest?
You will be paid daily or weekly depending on the pay-plan you choose.

- When will I get my first payment?
Depending on when you chose to be paid, you will be paid one day or one week from the day you make your deposit. Every time you make a NEW spend, you will receive payouts for that spend individually!

- When will I get my principal back?
  • Plan A: Your principal will be returned to you within 2 days of your final interest payment.
  • Plan B: Your principal will be returned to you within 2 days of your final interest payment.
  • Plan C: Your principal will be returned to you within 2 days of your final interest payment.
- What is the minimum and maximum investment?
The minimum investment is $250 for Plan A, $250 for Plan B and $1000 for Plan C. There is no maximum.

- Can I make additional deposits after my initial deposit is made?
Yes, you can add additional funds to your account at any time. (minimum $250 for Plan A, $250 for Plan B and $1000 for Plan C) Every time you make a NEW spend, you will receive payouts for that spend individually!

- How do I get credit for my referrals?
You ask your referrals to type your egold or evocash account number in the referrer area when sending their deposit.

- When will I be paid for my referrals?
You will be paid for your referrals within 48 hours of the person you referred having made their deposit.

- Can I receive referral bonus without investing any money?
Yes, you can. Just use your e-gold or evo-cash account number as a Sponsor Id
